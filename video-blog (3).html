My Journey

Naphtali Bryant is a dynamic speaker, award-winning educator, and recognized intrapreneur dedicated to transforming education into a launchpad for career success. His journey began with a bold decision: after a 13-year gap in schooling, he returned to college, determined to move beyond retail and into a career of purpose.

That decision launched a path of high achievement, including multiple degrees, national scholarships, and international study experiences in Taiwan and China. In 2017, Naphtali founded Spark-ED University — an initiative equipping students to uncover their purpose, secure funding with little to no debt, and gain early career opportunities.
